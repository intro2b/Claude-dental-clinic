About BrightSmile

A trusted dental home for Delhi families since 2009. Driven by compassion, guided by expertise.

📖 Our Story

15 Years of Caring for Delhi Smiles

BrightSmile Dental Care was founded in 2009 by Dr. Anil Mehta with a simple yet powerful mission — to make quality dental care accessible, painless, and affordable for every family in Delhi.

Over the past 15 years, we've grown from a small neighbourhood clinic into one of Rajouri Garden's most trusted dental practices, now serving 5,000+ happy patients across West Delhi.

Today, BrightSmile is home to a team of three specialized dentists who share a common belief: every patient deserves world-class care in a warm, stress-free environment where they feel heard, respected, and comfortable.
