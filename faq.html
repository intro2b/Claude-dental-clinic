Common questions from our patients about treatments, costs, and visiting BrightSmile.

We use the latest painless techniques and modern anesthesia so most patients feel little to no discomfort during treatment. Our team is specially trained to make anxious patients feel at ease throughout the procedure. Many of our root canal patients are genuinely surprised at how comfortable the experience is!
Yes, we work with most major health insurance providers in India. Our team will help you verify your insurance coverage and handle the paperwork for eligible treatments. Please call us at +91 98765 43210 or WhatsApp us to confirm whether your specific plan is accepted before your visit.
We recommend a professional check-up and cleaning every 6 months for healthy individuals. However, if you have ongoing dental concerns, gum disease, or are undergoing active treatment, your dentist may recommend more frequent visits. Regular check-ups help catch issues early, saving you time, discomfort, and money in the long run.
Traditional braces are most effective between ages 12–18 when the jaw and teeth are still developing, but adults can absolutely get braces too! Invisalign clear aligners are a popular choice for adults and teenagers who prefer a discreet option. Dr. Anil Mehta will assess your specific case and recommend the best treatment during your free consultation.
Most root canals at BrightSmile are completed in a single sitting of 60–90 minutes, thanks to our use of advanced rotary endodontic technology. In complex cases with multiple canals or severe infection, a second appointment may be needed. Dr. Sneha Kapoor will give you a clear timeline during your consultation.
Dental implants at BrightSmile start at ₹25,000 per tooth, which includes the titanium implant, abutment, and porcelain crown. The exact cost varies based on the complexity of your case and the materials chosen. We also offer flexible EMI payment options to make implants accessible for everyone. Book a free consultation for a personalised quote.
Yes! Our professional laser teeth whitening is completely safe when performed by a qualified dentist. Unlike over-the-counter kits, we use clinically approved whitening agents at controlled concentrations. Some patients experience mild, temporary sensitivity after treatment, which usually subsides within 24 hours.
